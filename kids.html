Children:

Apple iPod Touch, can be bought on the Apple website. The iPod touch is essentially a mini iPhone, primarily made for music, created by Apple. It holds music, works more essentially via Wi-Fi, and is a good start off to technology for your kid. This is a good way to teach kids about how to use technology wisely, and to not get addicted. This comes in six colors, so customization is available.

The Nintendo 3DS, can be bought on the Nintendo website. The Nintendo 3DS is a handheld gaming device created by Nintendo. Mainly for playing games created by the company, this device is designed for handheld gaming on the go. Your child can keep themselves entertained by taking this everywhere they go. They will have fun for a long time. Nintendo 3DS is very popular amongst children, and highly recommended for yours. Enjoy a range of games such as the Mario franchise, Zelda, and much more.

The Nintendo Switch, this can be bought on Nintendo website. The Nintendo Switch is a new handheld gaming console also created by Nintendo. This is also to play many games primarily made by the company, as well as third-party games, such as Fortnite, and Stranger Things 3, The Game. You can play in 3 ways. Handheld mode if the person is using the Switch themselves, Tabletop mode for 2 players, and TV mode for displaying the game on a television. There are also controllers too for more gaming tactics, and there are different Joy-Con colors for customization.
